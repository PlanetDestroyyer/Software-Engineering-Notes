The Agile Model is a type of incremental model where software is developed in a rapid increment cycle. The most notable Feature of the Agile model is the establishment of the project scope , requierments , number and duration of iteration at the beginning of the development process

Feature :
> Used in Large Projects
> Changing requierments
> Continue Customer involvment
> Highly qualified team required
> Errors easy to recognized
> Flexiable timeline & Budget

Disadvantages :
> Poor Documentation
> Maintainance difficult
